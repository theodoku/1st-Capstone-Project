ORBIT WEB INNOVATIONS

Orbit Web Innovations is a programming school located in Oslo, Norway. Our mission is to educate ethical, entrepreneurial leaders in software programming; to cultivate within students the critical thinking skills, the concern for others, and the courage it will take to transform the continent. Plenty of on-campus (and online) opportunities are available for you to get hands-on learning experience and work on projects with classmates, too. One of the best things about studying technology is the sheer volume of opportunities open to you once you graduate.
